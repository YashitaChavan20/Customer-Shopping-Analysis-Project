# Customer-Shopping-Analysis-Project
===============================================

## Project Overview
---------------------
This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior using a cleaned retail dataset to uncover actionable insights related to **revenue, customer demographics, subscriptions, purchase frequency, discounts, and product performance**.

The analysis is performed end-to-end using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**, resulting in a **single-page, insight-driven dashboard** designed for business decision-making.

---

## Business Objective
-----------------------
The primary objective of this project is to answer key business questions such as:

- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Do subscribed customers spend more?
- Which products and categories drive the most revenue?
- How do discounts impact purchasing behavior?
- What customer segments show strong loyalty?

## Key Insights & Analysis
----------------------------

### Customer Demographics
- Middle-aged and Adult customers contribute the highest revenue.
- Female customers generate a larger share of total revenue compared to male customers.

### Customer Segmentation
- Customers are segmented into **New, Returning, and Loyal** based on previous purchases.
- A large proportion of customers fall into the **Loyal** segment, indicating strong retention.

### Subscription Impact
- Subscribed customers have a **higher average spend**.
- Non-subscribers still contribute significant revenue, highlighting an opportunity for subscription upselling.

### Purchase Behavior
- Express and Free Shipping are the most preferred delivery options.
- Frequent buyers (weekly/fortnightly) prefer digital payment methods.

### Product & Discount Insights
- Clothing and Accessories dominate total purchases.
- Certain products contribute a high percentage of **discounted revenue**, indicating price sensitivity.
- Top 3 products within each category were identified using ranking logic.
